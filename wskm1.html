Stay aware and vigilant

One of the most important safety tips is to stay aware of your surroundings. Whether you are walking alone at night or in a crowded place, staying alert can help you notice suspicious behavior and react accordingly. Avoid distractions like looking at your phone or wearing headphones in unfamiliar or less secure areas. Trust your instincts; if something feels off, it probably is. Walk confidently and make eye contact with people around you, which can deter potential attackers.
