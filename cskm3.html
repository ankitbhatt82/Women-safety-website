Create a family safety plan

Having a well-thought-out safety plan helps children know what to do in various emergency situations. Steps to create a family safety plan include:

Emergency Contacts: Teach children how to dial emergency numbers and make sure they know their home address and phone numbers of parents or guardians.

Practice Fire Drills: Regularly practice fire drills at home so children know how to quickly and safely exit the house. Identify at least two ways out of each room and a safe meeting place outside.

Discuss Natural Disasters: Depending on your location, educate your children on what to do in case of natural disasters like earthquakes, tornadoes, or floods. Practice the appropriate responses, such as "Drop, Cover, and Hold On" for earthquakes or going to a basement for tornadoes.
