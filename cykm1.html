Use strong and unique password

Using strong, unique passwords for your online accounts is one of the most critical steps in protecting your personal information. A strong password typically includes a mix of upper and lower case letters, numbers, and special characters. Avoid using easily guessable information like birthdays, common words, or sequences. Consider using a password manager to generate and store complex passwords securely.
