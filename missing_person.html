Found Missing Person

What to Do:

If you have found a missing person, it's crucial to take immediate action:

Notify Authorities: Contact local law enforcement or the nearest police station to report the found individual.

Provide Details: Provide as much information as possible about the person's appearance, clothing, and any identifying features.

Stay with the Person: If safe to do so, stay with the found person until help arrives.

Do Not Interrogate: Avoid asking the person too many questions, especially if they seem distressed. Let the authorities handle the situation.

Cooperate with Authorities: Work closely with law enforcement to provide any necessary information or assistance in the investigation.
