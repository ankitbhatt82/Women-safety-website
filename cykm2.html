Be cautious with personal information

Be mindful of the personal information you share online. Avoid posting sensitive information such as your home address, phone number, or financial details on social media platforms. Cybercriminals can use this information for identity theft or harassment. Always adjust your privacy settings to ensure that your personal information is visible only to people you trust.
