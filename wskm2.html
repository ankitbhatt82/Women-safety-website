Carry self-defense tools

Carrying self-defense tools like pepper spray, a personal alarm, or a whistle can provide an added layer of security. These tools are effective in deterring attackers and attracting attention if you find yourself in danger. Make sure you know how to use them properly. Keep these tools easily accessible, such as in your purse or on a keychain, so you can quickly grab them if needed. Additionally, consider taking a self-defense class to learn how to protect yourself in various situations.
