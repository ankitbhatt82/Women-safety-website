Enable two-factor authentication

Two-factor authentication (2FA) adds an extra layer of security to your online accounts. With 2FA, even if someone obtains your password, they will still need a second form of verification, such as a code sent to your mobile device, to access your account. This significantly reduces the risk of unauthorized access and helps protect your personal and financial information from cyber threats.
